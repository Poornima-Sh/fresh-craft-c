What We Do

Our Expertise

World-class manufacturing capabilities across every aspect of kidswear production.

01

Screen & Digital Printing

From bold graphic tees to delicate character prints — we support rotary, flat-screen, and high-definition digital printing for every kidswear category.

02

All-Over Printing

Edge-to-edge vibrant AOP using reactive dyes and sublimation — delivering seamless, full-coverage patterns that stay vivid wash after wash.

03

Embroidery

Precision multi-head computerised embroidery — logos, badges, character motifs — with capabilities from flat stitch to 3D puff embroidery.

04

Garment Washing

Stone wash, enzyme wash, acid wash, vintage & distressed effects — achieving the perfect hand-feel and aesthetics for denims and casual wear.

05

Surface Treatments

Foil printing, flock, crinkle, burnout, appliqué, sequins, rhinestones and heat-transfer — every surface treatment for premium garment finishing.

06

Quality Control

In-line inspection, pre-shipment AQL checks, lab testing for safety standards — our QC team ensures every piece meets international children's wear regulations.
